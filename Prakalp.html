PRAKALP:

Project Competition

Prizes:
1st: ₹ 10,000
2nd: ₹ 7500
3rd: ₹ 5000

Registration Fee: ₹
₹ 100 per Participant (after being shortlisted)


Theme:


The project model should be in the following field of engineering.
1. Mechanical 2. Civil / Environmental 3. Electrical / E&TC 4. Computer Science & Engineering / Data Science / AIML / Computer Science & Business Systems/ MCA 5. Biotechnology

General Rules and Guidelines for Competition:


1. The competition is open for UG, Diploma and PG (MCA) students with a valid student ID card of their college/university. 2. Each team should have compulsory 2 members and maximum up to 6 members. 3. Project can be in the form of a working model / software simulation / experimental setup / poster or prototype with abstract and references. 4. Project details (Subject and Abstract) should be sent to kitprakalp@gmail.com in the following format ( Participantname_collegename_Field ). 5. Project abstract must be limited to 300 words . 6. Deadline for submission (Abstract) is 15th February 2025; the acceptance shall be communicated by 18th February 2025 (via mail / WhatsApp). 7. Fee: Rs.100/- participation fees per head. 8. Spot entry is not allowed.






About Us

PIONEER, a National-level technical event organised by KITCoEK under ISTE (Indian Society for Technical Education). The Pioneer, in its 27th edition, stands as the flagship event of KITCoEK with a rich legacy spanning over 26 years. Two central events are Abhivyati and Prakalp. Along with two central events, Pioneer features nine departmental events, each meticulously organized by different departmental student associations.
