PIONEER

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System.   And ISTE student chapter in KITCoEK, just like every year this year too has arranged “PIONEER”.

PIONEER is the bridge between students and their technical skills. It is the a national level event organized by the students of KITCoEK. It is a platform for creative minds to showcase their innovational skills. It is a foundation where students get to experience various aspects of life.

    About Us

    PIONEER, a National-level technical event organised by KITCoEK under ISTE (Indian Society for Technical Education). The Pioneer, in its 27th edition, stands as the flagship event of KITCoEK with a rich legacy spanning over 26 years. Two central events are Abhivyati and Prakalp. Along with two central events, Pioneer features nine departmental events, each meticulously organized by different departmental student associations.
